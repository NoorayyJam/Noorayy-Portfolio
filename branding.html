Green Land

Brand Psychology

Earthy greens and calm browns convey growth, trust, and sustainability. Rounded typography communicates friendliness, while structured elements imply reliability.

Case Study

The client aimed to refresh their gardening-tools brand. Research revealed nature lovers respond to calm tones and minimalistic forms. The new identity balances serenity with modern usability.

Brand Elements

  • Typography: Bernard MT Condensed
  • Palette: #0A541B, #35220C, #0D8424, #333338, #000000
  • Mockups: packaging, signage, digital
